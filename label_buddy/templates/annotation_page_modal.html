How to annotate
Select a label and drug on the waveform in order to create a region. The region created will be labeled with the selected label and you will be able to see the region's starting and ending point to the left of your screen. You can delete or play selected regions by clicking on them and after clicking the play/delete buttons which appear under the waveform.

Save your annotation by clicking the Save button.

View the comment (if review exists) of the reviewer by clicking the Review button

Delete your annotation (if exists) by clicking the Delete button.

Click Next to start annotating the next unlabeled task.
